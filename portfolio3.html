Project Overview

The Product

GearUp is an intuitive gaming equipment website that provides a curated selection of advanced gaming gear, comprehensive product reviews, and fast shipping. It is built to support both serious and casual gamers with a responsive design, accessible interface, and a focus on delivering a hassle-free, informed shopping journey.

Project information

  • Category: Responsive Web
  • Project Duration: March 2025 - June, 2025

PROBLEM

Gamers often struggle to find reliable, high-quality equipment and trustworthy reviews in one place. Many existing platforms lack detailed product insights, smooth navigation, and responsive customer service, leading to a frustrating shopping experience for both competitive and casual gamers.

THE GOAL

TTo create a fast, user-friendly, and informative eCommerce platform that helps gamers easily discover, compare, and purchase gaming equipment with confidence, while also offering responsive customer support and a seamless shopping experience.

ROLE

UX/UI Designer. [UI (user interface), UX (user experience)] frontend developer

Responsibilities

User research, User persona, Problem statement, User journey map, User flow, Storyboard, Low fidelity wireframe, Low fidelity prototypes, High fidelity mockups, High fidelity prototypes, Usability studies

Accessibility Considerations

  1. Interactive elements like "Buy Now" and "View Details" use meaningful labels so that screen reader users know exactly what action each element performs .
  2. The site uses a consistent structure and intuitive navigation to help all users, including those with cognitive disabilities, understand and interact with the content easily.
  3. All input fields in forms (e.g., checkout, search) include visible labels and accessible error messages to support users with visual or cognitive challenges .

Going Forward

Impact

Users shared that the website made upgrading their gaming setup feel accessible and exciting. One quote from peer feedback was, "The gaming equipment website takes the hassle out of finding the best gear, making it easy to enhance my gaming experience with top-quality products and insightful reviews.

What I learned

TI learned that even though the problem I was trying to solve was a big one, diligently going through each step of the design process and aligning with specific user needs helped me come up with solutions that were both feasible and useful.
